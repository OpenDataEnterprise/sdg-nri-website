The Center for Open Data Enterprise collaborates with public, private and nonprofit organizations that have a shared interest in utilizing data to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals. CODE welcomes interest from national statistical offices (NSOs), related statistical and data institutions, and international organizations to further advance SDG national reporting.

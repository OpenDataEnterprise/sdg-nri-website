The SDGs set out an ambitious agenda for countries around the world, and data is essential to help fulfill that agenda. To fulfill the UN data requirements for the 17 SDG goals, 169 targets and over 230 indicators, countries will need to ensure their reporting is public, derived from official data sources, standardized, and well-documented. And in deciding how to report on the SDGs, they will need to determine which technical approach best meets their needs and is sustainable through 2030.

This section includes resources to help countries assess their data resources, familiarize themselves with relevant data and metadata standards, and understand the various technical options available.

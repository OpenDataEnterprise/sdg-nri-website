As countries set their objectives for SDG reporting, they may evaluate the extent to which the SDGs relate to their national strategies for development and other policy priorities, including both sectoral strategies and policies and sub-national strategies and policies. They will also need to consider set-up and maintenance costs, and technical and human capacity requirements.

This section includes resources about policies, stakeholder engagement, financing and capacity relevant to SDG reporting.

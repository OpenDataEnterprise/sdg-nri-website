Most open-source platforms are developed using public repositories for code and data. The most common of these include Github, Gitlab, and Bitbucket. Utilizing such repositories will be central to any open-source SDG NRP project. Platform code itself will commonly be maintained on such a repository, and some platforms will also take advantage of hosting services.

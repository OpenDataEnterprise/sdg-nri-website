SDG reporting refers to the tracking of progress towards the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and targets. SDG reporting is closely associated with SDG monitoring - the assessment, collection, compilation and dissemination of official data and statistics on the SDGs.

Each country is responsible for its own data collection, monitoring, and review processes, with additional guidance and technical assistance provided through regional and international bodies.
